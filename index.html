cred flow

CredFlow is a SaaS-based cash flow management solution designed to help small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) automate and optimize their financial operations, particularly in the areas of accounts receivable and collections. It functions as an automated payment collection engine that integrates directly with popular accounting software like Tally and Busy ERP, enabling businesses to track outstanding invoices, send automated payment reminders via email, SMS, or calls, and receive timely payments.

tally customization

The term "tally" has multiple meanings depending on context. As a noun, it refers to a continuous count or record of something, such as points scored in a game, the number of items in inventory, or a running total of expenses. For example, a player's goal tally represents the total number of goals they have scored. As a verb, "to tally" means to calculate or add up numbers, such as tallying the cost of a project , or to ensure that two accounts, figures, or statements agree with each other.
